# Vertex Living — Google Business Profile Setup Guide (50 Cities)

## Overview
You need to create **50 separate Google Business Profile listings** — one for each city.
Each listing will help you rank in the **Google Maps 3-Pack** for "rent flat [city]" searches.

---

## Quick Start Link
**👉 https://business.google.com/locations?create=true**

You'll create listings one by one. Save this guide and follow the steps below for each city.

---

## For Each City — Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Create New Listing
1. Go to https://business.google.com/locations?create=true
2. Click **"Manage Now"** if not logged in — login with your Google account
3. Click **"Add your business"**

### Step 2: Business Info (fill for EACH city)

For every city, use this template — just replace **[CITY]** with the actual city name:

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Business Name** | `Vertex Living — Rentals in [CITY]` |
| **Category** | `Real Estate Rental Agency` |
| **Address** | *(see "Address Strategy" below)* |
| **Phone** | `+91 9671009931` (same for all) |
| **Website** | `https://vertexliving.in/rent/[city-slug]` |

### Step 3: Address Strategy
Since you operate pan-India without physical offices in every city:

**Option A — Use your main office:**
Address: `Plot 5, Sector 42, Gurugram, Haryana, India`
This single address works for all 50 listings.

**Option B — Use "Service Area" mode:**
1. During setup, select **"Yes, I also serve customers at my business address"**
2. Then check **"I serve customers at locations other than my address"**
3. Add all cities as service areas: "All cities in India, focusing on [CITY]"

### Step 4: Verification
- Google will send a **postcard** to the address (5-7 business days)
- OR choose **phone verification** if available (instant)
- Enter the code to verify each listing

### Step 5: Complete Profile
Fill these for MAXIMUM visibility:

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Hours** | Monday-Saturday: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM |
| **Description** | `Vertex Living is India's leading zero-brokerage property rental platform. Find verified 2BHK, 3BHK flats, villas & PGs for rent across [CITY] — direct from owners, zero brokerage. 15+ years experience, RERA verified properties.` |
| **Photos** | Upload logo + hero rental property images |
| **Attributes** | Check: "Online appointments", "On-site services" |

## FAQ
**Q: Can I use the same address for all 50?**
A: Yes. Use your main office address or any valid address. Google allows one address for service-area businesses.

**Q: Do I need to verify each one?**
A: Yes, each listing needs verification. Phone verification is faster than postcard.

**Q: How long until they appear in Google Maps?**
A: Typically 1-2 weeks after verification. Some may take up to 30 days.

**Q: Can I manage all 50 from one dashboard?**
A: Yes! Use Google Business Profile Manager: https://business.google.com/locations
